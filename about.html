The WTFIX Platform

Where The Fuck Is Xûr (WTFIX) is evolving into a community-driven project for the Destiny™ players. The site is being developed as an open-source platform — a source for information on Xûr and his fellow vendors across the entire solar system. Our goal is to be a premier source of vendor information, current events, and news in the Destiny™ universe. These updates will be delivered in the best (and most entertaining) ways possible: on our website, through our telephone number, and across other delivery platforms currently under development.
